Product Building Challenge

Product Building Challenge at Freshworks STS Software Academy

The challenge was to build a creative and useful product with a budget of ₹500. After brainstorming many ideas and multiple reviews, our product idea “Portable Desk” was approved.

Our squad, Virtual Warriors, divided responsibilities and worked collaboratively to implement the product. We also focused on branding by designing a logo, pamphlets, and stickers to make the product market-ready.

On the final day, we presented the product to external guests from Freshworks, received valuable feedback, and secured Second Prize. This experience was a proud and memorable milestone.

Photo Essay Project

Field Study & Visual Storytelling Activity

During one of our academy tasks, our mentor gave us four project options. I chose the Photo Essay task, even though I was not fully sure what it meant at first.

With guidance, we researched and understood that a photo essay tells a meaningful story through visuals.

We were assigned to visit DakshinaChitra, Muttukadu, Chennai, a heritage museum. I chose the theme Traditional Vessels and photographed vessels representing four South Indian states.

I compiled the photographs into a structured presentation. This experience improved my observation skills, creativity, and ability to think about angles and visual storytelling.
